What is PSARA?

The Private Security Agencies Regulation Act (PSARA) governs the licensing and regulation of private security agencies in India. Every security agency must hold a valid PSARA license to operate legally. Our consultancy helps agencies navigate this process — from documentation to final approval — in a transparent and timely manner.
